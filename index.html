How Do You Make the Best Chocolate Cake?

  • Make sure to mix everything properly with no lumps.
  • Adjust cooking times based on your oven's power.

    • The Key Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder

    • Baking Instructions:

    1. Prehat oven to 350 degrees
    2. Grease a 9x9 pan
    3. Cream together butter and sugar until no lumps.
    4. Add eggs and vanilla extract to the mixture and combine until smooth
    5. Add all the dry ingredients until fully combined
    6. Add milk slowly until everything is smooth and creamy
    7. Pour batter into greased pan
    8. Put it into the oven and bake for 22 minutes
    9. Pull out when golden brown at the edges and let cool before taking out of the pan
    10. Enjoy and share with others!
